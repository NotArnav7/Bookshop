the greatest ever book shop in the world

Bookshop is an online bookstore with a mission to financially support local, independent bookstores. We believe that bookstores are essential to a healthy culture. They’re where authors can connect with readers, where we discover new writers, where children get hooked on the thrill of reading that can last a lifetime. They’re also anchors for our downtowns and communities.

Doglapan: The Hard Truth about Life and Start-Ups
This book is the story of Startup India’s favorite poster boy, Ashneer Grover, an honest and raw-at-heart kid.

In this book, there is the story of Startup India promoter Ashneer Grover, all the things that have happened in his life from childhood till now have been told. Along with this, he has told how he has faced difficulties in life. A young boy from Malviya Nagar, Delhi becomes an academic rank holder from the Indian Institute of Delhi and then goes to the Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad to do an MBA. After that, he made his career by investing in banks like Kotak and AmEx. In the initial chapters, he told about the initial circumstances of his life in which he could not afford his expenses. Along with this, he also had to negotiate for the salary. Also, mention the experience with different companies.
